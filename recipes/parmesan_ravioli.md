
    # Parmesan Ravioli

    Today I'm presenting you a really easy recipe with an absolutly amazing taste!

For the ravioli dough:
700g semolina
300g flour
600g egg yolk

Knead and vacuum for 3 hours.

For the Ravioli filling:
300g Parmesan matured for 36 months
100g cream or milk
Pepper
Xanthan
